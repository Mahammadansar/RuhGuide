UPI QR Code Setup Instructions
==============================

1. Generate Your UPI QR Code:
   - Open your UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, etc.)
   - Go to "Receive Money" or "Your QR Code" section
   - Save/download the QR code image

2. Add to Project:
   - Save the QR code image as: upi-qr-code.png
   - Place it in the /public folder
   - Recommended size: 512x512 pixels or larger
   - Format: PNG with transparent background (optional)

3. Update UPI ID:
   - Open components/DonationButton.tsx
   - Replace "yourupi@paytm" with your actual UPI ID
   - Open app/about/page.tsx
   - Replace "yourupi@paytm" with your actual UPI ID

4. Test:
   - The QR code should appear in the donation popup
   - Users can scan it with any UPI app
   - The "Copy UPI ID" button will copy your UPI ID

Example UPI IDs format:
- yourname@paytm
- yourname@ybl (PhonePe)
- yourname@okaxis (Axis Bank)
- yourname@okhdfcbank (HDFC Bank)
- yourname@upi (BHIM)
